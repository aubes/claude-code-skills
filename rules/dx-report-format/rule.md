When producing a review, audit, research summary, or any report with findings, use these severity indicators:

- ❌ Critical / Blocker
- ⚠️ High / Warning
- 💡 Medium / Suggestion
- ℹ️ Info / Low
- ✅ Positive / OK

Map severity words from skills and audits to these indicators automatically (e.g. "Critical" becomes ❌, "Warning" becomes ⚠️).

Lead each finding with its indicator so the reader can scan quickly.
